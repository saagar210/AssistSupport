# Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy

**Status:** ACTIVE
**Classification:** MANDATORY
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-12

## Policy Statement

Personal devices may be used for work purposes ONLY when enrolled in the company Mobile Device Management (MDM) system and meeting all security requirements.

## Requirements for Personal Devices

1. **MDM enrollment**: Device must be enrolled in Intune/Jamf
2. **Encryption**: Full disk encryption must be enabled
3. **Passcode**: 6+ digit PIN or biometric lock required
4. **OS updates**: Must be within 30 days of latest OS version
5. **Antivirus**: Company-approved antivirus must be installed (Windows)
6. **Remote wipe**: Must consent to remote wipe capability

## Prohibited

- Jailbroken or rooted devices
- Devices running unsupported OS versions
- Devices without encryption capability
- Shared family devices
