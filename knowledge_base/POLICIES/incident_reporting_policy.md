# Security Incident Reporting Policy

**Status:** ACTIVE
**Classification:** MANDATORY
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-14

## Policy Statement

All security incidents, suspected breaches, and policy violations must be reported immediately to the IT Security team. Failure to report is itself a violation.

## What to Report

- Lost or stolen devices (laptops, phones, tablets)
- Suspected phishing emails or social engineering
- Unauthorized access to systems or data
- Malware or ransomware alerts
- Accidental data exposure
- Policy violations you witness
- Suspicious physical access to secure areas

## How to Report

1. **Urgent**: Call the IT Security hotline (ext. 5555)
2. **Non-urgent**: Email security@company.com
3. **Anonymous**: Use the anonymous reporting portal on the intranet
4. **Phishing**: Forward suspicious emails to phishing@company.com

## Response Time

- Critical incidents: Response within 15 minutes
- High severity: Response within 1 hour
- Medium: Response within 4 hours
- Low: Response within 1 business day
