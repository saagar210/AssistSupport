# Acceptable Use Policy

**Status:** ACTIVE
**Classification:** MANDATORY
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-10

## Policy Statement

Company IT resources are provided for business purposes. Limited personal use is permitted provided it does not violate security policies, interfere with work, or consume excessive resources.

## Acceptable Use

- Business-related work
- Professional development and training
- Brief personal use during breaks (web browsing, personal email)

## Prohibited Use

- Accessing inappropriate or illegal content
- Cryptocurrency mining
- Unauthorized data collection or scraping
- Installing unauthorized software
- Bypassing security controls (proxies, VPN circumvention)
- Using company resources for personal business
- Excessive bandwidth consumption (streaming, large downloads)
- Harassment or discrimination via company systems

## Monitoring

Company reserves the right to monitor all activity on company-owned devices and networks. This includes email, web browsing, and file access.
