# Email and Communication Security Policy

**Status:** ACTIVE
**Classification:** MANDATORY
**Last Updated:** 2025-01-06

## Policy Statement

Company email must be used for all work communications. Sensitive data must be encrypted. Phishing attempts must be reported immediately.

## Requirements

- Use company email for all business communication
- Enable encryption for emails containing sensitive data (use the "Encrypt" button in Outlook)
- Report suspicious emails to phishing@company.com
- Do not forward company emails to personal accounts
- Do not auto-forward email to external addresses

## Prohibited

- Using personal email for work communications
- Sending sensitive/confidential data via unencrypted email
- Clicking links in suspicious emails
- Opening unexpected attachments
- Sharing email credentials
