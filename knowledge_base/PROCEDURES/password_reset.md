# Password Reset Procedure

## Self-Service Password Reset

1. Go to https://passwordreset.company.com
2. Enter your company email address
3. Verify your identity via MFA (push notification or SMS)
4. Create a new password meeting requirements:
   - 12+ characters
   - Uppercase + lowercase + number + special character
   - Cannot reuse last 12 passwords
5. Click "Reset Password"
6. Sign out of all devices and sign back in with new password

## If Self-Service Fails

1. Contact IT Helpdesk at ext. 1234
2. Verify your identity (employee ID + manager name)
3. IT will issue a temporary password
4. You must change it on first login

## Account Lockout

After 5 failed login attempts, your account is locked for 30 minutes. If urgent, contact IT Helpdesk to unlock immediately.
