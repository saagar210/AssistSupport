# How to Report a Technical Issue

## Steps

1. **Go to the IT Helpdesk Portal** at https://it.company.com/support
2. **Click "New Ticket"**
3. **Select the category** that best matches your issue:
   - Hardware (laptop, monitor, keyboard, mouse)
   - Software (application errors, crashes, installation)
   - Network (Wi-Fi, VPN, internet connectivity)
   - Account (login issues, password reset, access requests)
   - Email (Outlook, calendar, email delivery)
   - Other
4. **Describe the issue** in detail:
   - What were you trying to do?
   - What happened instead?
   - When did it start?
   - Any error messages? (screenshot if possible)
5. **Set priority**:
   - Critical: Cannot work at all (system down, no access)
   - High: Major feature not working, workaround available
   - Medium: Minor issue, can still work
   - Low: Enhancement or question
6. **Submit the ticket**

## Response Times

- Critical: 15 minutes
- High: 1 hour
- Medium: 4 hours
- Low: 1 business day

## Escalation Process

1. **Level 1**: IT Helpdesk (initial triage and resolution)
2. **Level 2**: Specialized IT team (network, security, application)
3. **Level 3**: Engineering/vendor escalation
4. **Manager escalation**: Contact your manager or IT manager if SLA is missed
