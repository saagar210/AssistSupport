# How to Request a New Laptop

## Overview
All laptop requests go through the IT Asset Management portal. Manager approval is required.

## Steps

1. **Log into the IT Self-Service Portal** at https://it.company.com/assets
2. **Select "New Device Request"** from the catalog
3. **Choose your device type**: Laptop (Standard), Laptop (Developer), Laptop (Executive)
4. **Fill in the business justification** — explain why you need a new device
5. **Submit for manager approval** — your direct manager will receive an email
6. **Manager approves** — typically within 1-2 business days
7. **IT procurement orders the device** — standard models ship within 5 business days
8. **IT configures the device** — security software, encryption, VPN, MDM enrollment
9. **Pickup or delivery** — IT will contact you to arrange handoff
10. **Old device return** — return your current device within 5 business days of receiving the new one

## Available Models

- **Standard**: Dell Latitude 5540 (16GB RAM, 512GB SSD)
- **Developer**: MacBook Pro 14" (32GB RAM, 1TB SSD) or Dell Precision 5570
- **Executive**: MacBook Air 15" (24GB RAM, 512GB SSD)

## Timeline

- Manager approval: 1-2 business days
- Procurement: 3-5 business days (standard models)
- Configuration: 1 business day
- **Total: 5-8 business days for standard models**

## Escalation

If your request is urgent (broken/lost device), contact the IT helpdesk directly at ext. 1234.
